# Security & Protection

With the release of **v1.4**, SoundFlow introduces a dedicated `SoundFlow.Security` namespace. This suite of tools is designed to address the growing needs of modern audio applications: protecting intellectual property, ensuring content authenticity, and identifying audio assets.

We call this the **Secure Audio Pipeline**. It allows you to protect audio assets from the moment they are recorded or generated, all the way through distribution and playback, without ever exposing the raw PCM data to unauthorized access.

## The Four Pillars of Security

SoundFlow's security architecture is built on four distinct but complementary pillars, each serving a specific role in the asset lifecycle.
